# TrainHealth – Track Your Fitness, Progress, and Performance

TrainHealth is your all-in-one companion to monitor and improve your physical performance. Designed for fitness enthusiasts of all levels, TrainHealth simplifies how you track your workouts, body metrics, and progress over time — so you can focus on training smarter.

WORKOUTS – STRUCTURE AND PERFORMANCE

-	Start training sessions directly from your iPhone or Apple Watch.
-	Create and manage a personalized library of exercises.
-	Record your session directly from the app.
-	Log sets, reps, and weights in real-time during your workout.
-	Automatically sync your workouts with Apple Health.

WEIGHT TRACKING – STAY ON TRACK

-	Visualize your weight evolution over the last 12 months.
-	Data is automatically retrieved from Apple Health.

BODY MEASUREMENTS – TRACK PHYSICAL CHANGES

- Record key measurements (arms, shoulders, thighs, and more).
- Monitor your physical progress with intuitive graphs.

## ✅ Free Version

# 📋 General Privacy

### What kind of information is collected?

**Minimal and secure.**

TrainHealth stores your data privately using **Apple’s CloudKit**. This ensures your information stays within Apple’s secure ecosystem and is **never shared with third parties**.

---

# 🔍 Personal Data

TrainHealth does **not** collect or request any of the following:

- Name  
- Gender  
- Email address  
- Phone number  
- Address  
- Or any personally identifying information outside of Apple’s platform

Your **training data** — workouts, weight logs, and body measurements — are stored either **locally** on your device or **privately in your iCloud account**, depending on your usage.

This data is **never accessible by the developer**, and is only used to power the core features of the app.

---

# 🧠 Health Data

If you choose to connect TrainHealth to **Apple Health**, the app will access and write data related to:

- Workouts  
- Body weight  
- Other fitness-related metrics

This connection is fully controlled by you via your device’s **Health permissions**.

None of your Health data is ever sent outside of your device or your iCloud.

---

# 🔐 Personally-Identifying Information

TrainHealth does not transmit or store any personally-identifying information outside of Apple’s secure platforms.  
All data stays:

- On your device  
- In your private iCloud storage (via CloudKit)  
- Inside Apple Health (if enabled by you)

Only you have access to this data.

# 💳 For Subscribers

If you subscribe to **TrainHealth Pro**, all billing is managed **entirely through the App Store**.
